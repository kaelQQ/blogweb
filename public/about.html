About Monolog

Monolog is a web project developed by Data Science students from the University of Science and Technology of the Southern Philippines. Created as a partial fulfillment of the Web Development course under the guidance of instructor Mark Gaje, this platform reflects the students' applied learning and collaborative effort in building functional, user-centered web applications.

Our Mission

To provide a simple yet powerful platform for individuals to express their thoughts and share their stories with the world.

Our Vision

To create a community where ideas flow freely and knowledge is shared without boundaries.
